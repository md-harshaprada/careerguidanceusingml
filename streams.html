Career Streams

    Agriculture, Food, and Natural Resources

Improving the quality and safety of food, developing and maintaining our natural resources, and caring for animals are all jobs in the Agriculture, Food, and Natural Resources cluster.This professional path is broken down into seven sections namely Agribusiness Systems,Animal Systems,Environmental Service Systems,Food Products & Processing Systems,Natural Resources Systems, Plant Systems and Power, Structural & Technical Systems

    Architecture and Construction

Architecture is the process of planning, designing, and constructing buildings or other structures, as well as the end result. Architectural works, in the form of structures, are frequently regarded as cultural emblems and works of art. The process of creating a building or infrastructure is known as construction. Construction varies from manufacturing in that manufacturing often entails mass production of comparable things without a specific consumer, whereas construction is typically done on-site for a recognised client.

    Arts, Audio/Video Technology, and Communication

Courses and activities that challenge students' creative abilities are required for careers in the arts, audio/visual technology, and communications.There are six job paths in this cluster are Visual Arts,,Performing Arts,Journalism and Broadcasting,Audio/Video Technology,Print Technology and Telecommunications.

    Business and Finance

Business vocations are among the most in-demand and fastest-growing of all professions. One of the reasons for this is that business experts are required in almost every industry. Health care administrators, financial analysts, entertainment managers, retail marketing specialists, sales representatives, and restaurant consultants are just a few of the jobs available to business professionals. Another factor for the popularity of business occupations is the remuneration. Successful business professionals are among the world's highest-paid professionals. Executives, managers, and entrepreneurs may easily earn more than $150,000 per year.

    Education and Training

Education and training is a subject in which individuals are trained and guided using scientific approaches. It is the process of aiding information and skill development and learning.Teachers, school administrators, academic support, subject matter mentors, special needs experts, and academic advisers are all jobs in education. Curriculum consultants and designers, as well as specialists in educational content development, private tutors, private schools, and certificate programmes, are all examples of educational professionals. Corporate trainers, software trainers, speakers, conference providers, educational courseware developers, and implementation professionals are all vocations in the training family.

    Government and Public Administration

At the federal, state, and municipal levels, jobs in the government and public administration career cluster entail planning, supervising, and providing government legislative, administrative, and regulatory services, as well as associated general purpose government services. There are seven job paths in this cluster:Governance, National Security, Foreign Service, Planning, Revenue and Taxation, Regulation and Public Management and Administration

    Health Science

Human and animal health are the subjects of health sciences, which is a branch of applied sciences. The health sciences are separated into two categories: 1) is the study, research, and understanding of health; 2) is the application and execution of that knowledge to treat diseases and improve and enhance overall health care. In other words, the fundamental goal of this discipline is to investigate, apply, and create information and technology in order to improve patient diagnosis and treatment. The area of health sciences encompasses a wide range of fields that integrate science, engineering, and, most crucially, technology to provide human healthcare. Students with a degree in health science courses get a better grasp of the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of a variety of diseases.

    Information Technology

Information technology has penetrated and helped every industry area. IT experts are in high demand because of their skill and experience in designing, supporting, and maintaining computer hardware and software for a variety of industrial and personal applications. According to the National Association of Software and Service Companies, Indian software exports would expand by 12% to $112 billion in 2015-16. The scarcity of educated and competent IT personnel is likely the sole, or at least the most significant, impediment to the country attaining this growth rate.

Law, Public Safety, Corrections, and Security

The highly competent Law, Public Safety, Corrections, and Security Career Cluster offers tens of thousands of difficult educational and training options. In the fields of corporate, industrial, homeland security, and public safety, these opportunities are continuing to grow. There are five job paths in this career cluster are Emergency and fire management, Security and protective services, Law enforcement services, Legal services and Correction services

Marketing

A marketing job may lead you in a variety of areas. Marketing encompasses a wide range of actions and perspectives. There are several chances in marketing, but the common denominator among them is a sense of ownership over the product and/or service, as well as the requirement to understand the customer's wants and wishes and then be able to convert those demands into your marketing strategy's message. Marketing communication may be done in a variety of ways, which is why a career in marketing can lead to a variety of opportunities.
